How to Get to Puerto Viejo

Puerto Viejo is approximately 4 hours by car from San José, the capital of Costa Rica. You can get there by:

  • Own or rental car: Take Route 32 towards Limón and then Route 36 to Puerto Viejo.
  • Bus: Direct services from San José with companies like Autotransportes MEPE.
  • Private shuttle: Several companies offer door-to-door transfers from the airport.

Best Time to Visit

The South Caribbean has a different climate than Costa Rica's Pacific coast. The driest season is from February to April and from September to October. However, Puerto Viejo is beautiful year-round, and tropical rain is usually brief and intense, leaving the rest of the day clear.
