Welcome to
Sunshine Caribe

Sunshine Caribe is located 2 minutes walk from the beach and 5 minutes from downtown, in Puerto Viejo de Talamanca, on the black sand beaches of Limón province.

A paradise beach for lovers of the sea and the sounds of nature. We comply with Law 7.600 requirements, with 24-hour security cameras.

Each of our rooms is spacious and features a wide corridor to enjoy afternoons and evenings, watch the forest nature and listen to the song of the ocean waves.

Discover Puerto Viejo

Puerto Viejo de Talamanca is one of Costa Rica's most exotic destinations. Its black sand beaches, lush tropical rainforest, Afro-Caribbean culture and rich biodiversity make it a unique place in the world.
